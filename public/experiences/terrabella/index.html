Terra Bella

Terra Bella (now Planet) is a satellite-based Earth imaging service.

I joined Terra Bella (formerly Skybox) after its acquisition by Google. I owned a Java-based service that produced a satellite command stream based on constraints around imaging and comms opportunities. I also worked on integrating commercial TLE propagation code into Google systems. I worked to improve CI/CD, support rollbacks, conduct post-mortems, etc.

Seeing SkySats 4-7 launch with the team, and later accept our commands, was a highlight. After a year of integration work, Google sold Terra Bella to Planet.
