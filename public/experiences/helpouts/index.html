Helpouts

Problem: How can Google literally connect a query to an expert?

Google helpouts was Google’s attempt to turn video chat into a business platform. If a Google query for How to fix a toilet leak? failed, it could connect you to an actual human plumber who’d talk to you and walk you through the repair on a paid tutorial platform.

I worked on the android app, the internal stack serving its API, integration with Google search, and the referral system.

This project offered piano lessons, cooking, vacation advice and medical services in one app. All of this in one UX was too muddled and new for both the consumers and the providers. One-medical is an example of how to do this well in one vertical.

Despite these drawbacks, I suspect it would have been wildly successful if launched during a pandemic.
