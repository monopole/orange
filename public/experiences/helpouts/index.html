Helpouts

Google Helpouts was Google’s attempt to turn video chat into a small business platform. If a Google query (How to fix a leaking toilet?) failed, Google could offer the user a chance to buy time with a human expert, anywhere in the world.

I worked on the android app and the API it talked to, arranged for consumer and provider notifications, and did integration with Google search.

This project offered piano lessons, cooking, vacation advice and medical services in one app. All of this in one UX was too broad and new for both the consumers and the providers. One-medical is an example of how to do this well in one vertical. It would have been wildly successful had it launched during a pandemic, but didn’t cross the chasm in 2014.
