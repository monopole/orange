G+ Games

Google+ Games launched when FarmVille was in its prime.

I was TLM of 5-10 swe/qa/interns, coordinating with extended team of dozens engaged in various aspects of G+ (stream, auth, privacy, and api).

We built the most complex part of the G+ web client, as it had to mitigate the risk of allowing 3rd party apps to run on Google domains. To do so, we launched the first aspect of the G+ API to games developers (contact sharing), preceding what came second (activity stream listing) by over a year. We also launched the first use of OAuth2 at Google.
